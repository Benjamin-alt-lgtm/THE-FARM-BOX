Our Story

Born from a
simple belief.

Fresh food should be accessible to every Lagos family — without markup, without middlemen, without compromise.

The Beginning

From one farmer's field to 500+ families

The Farm Box started in 2023 when our founder, Tunde Okafor, noticed something broken: his uncle's farm in Ogun State was producing world-class tomatoes, but Lagos families were buying inferior, preservative-laced produce from middlemen at inflated prices.

The solution was obvious. Cut out the chain. Connect farmers directly to consumers. Package it beautifully. Deliver it fast.

What started as 10 boxes a week delivered from a small van is now 500+ happy households and businesses receiving fresh farm produce weekly across Lagos.

Our Values
